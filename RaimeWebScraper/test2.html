Pop 360 Shuriken
Other Names: 
Pop 180 Shuriken (TKT)

The Pop 360 Hyper starts in Frontside stance, leaves the ground off of both feet, rotates 180° in the air, then lands and finishes with an outside cresecent kick towards the target and lands in complete stance. This move is often the first Shuriken that trickers learn, and is vitally important for future shuriken variations.
